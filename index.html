What Even is FLAMES?

Born in schoolyards across the world, FLAMES is the original relationship calculator. Before dating apps, before "sliding into DMs," there was just you, your crush's name, and a piece of paper.

The rules are simple: write both names, cancel out the common letters, count what's left, and let the alphabet gods decide your fate. Are you Friends? Lovers? Married? Or (heaven forbid) Enemies?
